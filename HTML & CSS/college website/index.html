 Main Context

This is the website of Government College for Women M.A. Road Sringar. This college is the part of Cluster University Sringar which is formed bt integrating the five collges of Srinagar.

Cluster University of Srinagar comprises: Amar Singh College, Government College for Women m.a. road Srinagar, Sri Pratap College, Abdul Ahad Azad Memorial Institute and Institute of Advanced Studies.

Actually this college was initially for women students only but from the last 3 years 3 courses have been made co-ed. The University is incresing co-ed courses in college year by year. So far the co-ed courses till date are: Integrated Masters in Computer Applications,Honors in English and Mass Communication and Journalism. Although the no. of male students is very less but many male students are admitted every year.
